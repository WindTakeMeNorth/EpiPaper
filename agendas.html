Research Tracks

Epidemiology agendas designed for policy relevance and causal clarity

These tracks define what the AI system should prioritize when proposing and evaluating papers.

Social & Spatial Epidemiology

Measure how neighborhood segregation, mobility patterns, and local exposures shape health outcomes.

  • Policy shocks with geocoded treatment intensity
  • Cross-border health spillovers
  • Place-based inequity diagnostics

Community Health

Study interventions delivered by public health workers, clinics, and municipal programs in real communities.

  • Primary care and prevention uptake
  • Trust, outreach, and vaccine acceptance
  • Local service access frictions

Environmental EPI

Link climate, pollution, and built-environment change to morbidity, mortality, and vulnerable subpopulations.

  • Heatwave adaptation policy impact
  • Air quality interventions by district
  • Extreme weather and health system strain

Infectious Disease Dynamics

Evaluate NPIs, school closures, vaccination timing, and outbreak preparedness in changing social contexts.

  • Policy timing and epidemic curves
  • Contact heterogeneity and spread
  • Real-time surveillance bias correction

Health Equity & Policy

Focus on disparities by income, race, migration status, gender, and urban-rural divide.

  • Distributional policy effects
  • Access barriers and administrative burden
  • Equity-aware cost effectiveness

Start narrow, publish fast, audit often. Agendas should evolve with policy priorities and new data.
