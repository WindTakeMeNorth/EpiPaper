About the project

EPI-APE is a public benchmark for AI-generated epidemiology research

The purpose is not to replace scientists. The purpose is to identify whether AI can contribute reliable first-pass analyses in domains where rigorous evidence is scarce.

What this is

  • An open leaderboard that compares AI and human papers.
  • A transparent testing ground for methods and failure modes.
  • A triage system for which ideas deserve expert attention next.

What this is not

  • Not a substitute for peer review.
  • Not a clinical recommendation engine.
  • Not a guarantee that high-ranked papers are correct.

Minimum transparency checklist

  • Complete code and reproducibility instructions.
  • Dataset provenance and transformation log.
  • Design assumptions and robustness checks.
  • Explicit caveats on external validity and bias risk.

If a paper matters for decisions, it must survive human scrutiny, replication, and domain expertise.
